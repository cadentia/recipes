Ingredients
 - macaroni pasta
 - milk
 - butter
 - cheddar cheese
 - flour
 - salt and pepper
 - parmesan cheese

Directions
 1. Cook the pasta per packet instructions

 2. Heat milk in a saucepan

 3. Melt butter in a pan

 4. Add flour

 5. Stir and gradually add the milk

 6. Stir in the cheddar

 7. Mix pasta and sauce

 8. Cover with parmesan and brown on the grill
